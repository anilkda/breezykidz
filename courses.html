Programs

Our Programmes believe that children must learn through experiences of touching, moving, listening, seeing & hearing. ‘Curiosity’-which is the innate nature of every child is satisfied with a theme based integrated approach, led by enquiry based learning. Great emphasis is laid on developing the intrinsic motivation to learn, autonomy & self-discipline. With innovative methods like ‘Discovery time’, the children are introduced to concepts of problem solving & creative thinking in a number of interesting ways.

Courses

Playgroup

Playgroup provides a world to explore for the toddlers. Attractive plush toys, building blocks, crayons and rocking chairs, provide an ample opportunity for kids to have fun. They are introduced into a society of their own kind where they make new friends and build relationships.

Nursery

Here the kids learn to express themselves and gain self-confidence. It is here that their intellectual, emotional, social and physical development is focussed. They learn to concentrate and develop their listening and grasping ability through rhymes. It is their moments of joy and freedom.

Day Care

Day care is provided for kids who may be attending other schools or enrolled into our programme. This is for the families that seek year-round schedule for enrolling their kids, This is a great relief to working parents. Breezy Kidz provided suitable activities and extra learning opportunities for such kids.

LKG

Here the kids are introduced to formal leaning. The kids learn to communicate and develop their basic skills through creative activities and social interaction. Here kids develop their cognitive skills to read and write. Teachers actively participate in child’s learning and ensure their overall development.

UKG

This is the most formative year of the child. Teachers take special care to instil societal, cultural, and moral values along with formal teachings. It is during this period that the kids are prepared for upper formal education.
