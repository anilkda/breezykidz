ABOUT US

Happiness is when what you think, what you say, and what you do are in harmony!

Breezy Kidz

“Happiness is when what you think, what you say, and what you do are in harmony.” These are the words as told by Mahatma Gandhi. We at Breezy Kidz help child to grow with a curriculum which is very ‘child centric’, ‘age specific’ & ‘developmentally appropriate’. The curriculum is extremely dynamic & highly re-searched. The approach to learning is experiential and it ensures that the child’s developments in all domains are in line with the requirements of future formal schooling. Various early childhood pedagogies like the Montessori Method, Gardner’s Multiple Intelligences, Steiner’s Waldorf method, Reggio Emilia method, Kindergarten system etc have been integrated while formulating this curriculum.

Vision

  • To develop skills and provide opportunities which will enrich each child’s social, emotional, physical, intellectual, and creative abilities.
  • To inspire curiosity, independence and a life-long love of learning to help our children become contributing members of our community and the world.
  • Committed to the families we serve, we strive to give parents complete peace of mind.
  • To warmly look back at the children experience with us, and see it as a cherished part of their childhood.

Mission

To provide a quality structured educational program, to nurture learning and imagination for child’s growth, with equal emphasis on academic and social development.
